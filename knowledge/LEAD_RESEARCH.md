# Lead Research

Research a lead the way the beat researches a story. To the source, no guessing.

## Find and verify from real pages
- What they do, size, locations, revenue model.
- Ownership and leadership, the people who would actually decide.
- The best verifiable CONTACT. Prefer a named leader's public business email,
  then a general business email, then a contact form or LinkedIn. NEVER invent or
  pattern-guess an address (no assuming info@domain). If nothing is verifiable,
  the lead goes to Talon by hand with a note.
- Competitors in their Alaska market.
- Their real pains, in their words where you can find them. Reviews, job posts,
  their own site, local news, the docket.
- What competitors are already doing with AI.

## Sourcing discipline
- Every fact in the dossier carries a source URL.
- Fetch the actual page before citing it. Do not trust a snippet.
- Note confidence. Flag anything thin rather than dressing it up.

## Good sources for Alaska
- Company sites and their careers and about pages.
- Alaska Business Magazine, Alaska Beacon, ADN, local outlets.
- ANCSA Regional Association and corporation sites for Native corporations.
- The Alaska AI docket for who is making AI and infrastructure moves.
- LinkedIn for leadership names and roles.
